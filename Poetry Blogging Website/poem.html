Featured Poems

                

                    

                        
Whispers of the Wind

                        

                            The wind whispers secrets

                            Through rustling leaves

                            A gentle melody

                            That the heart receives
                        

                        
                    

                    

                        
Moonlit Dreams

                        

                            Silver beams dance

                            On midnight's stage

                            Dreams take flight

                            As stars engage
                        

                        
                    

                    

                        
Autumn's Song

                        

                            Golden leaves fall

                            In a graceful dance

                            Nature's farewell

                            To summer's romance
                        

                        
                    

                    

                        
Ocean's Embrace

                        

                            Waves crash and roll

                            In endless motion

                            The sea's deep soul

                            A timeless ocean
                        

                        
                    

                    

                        
Morning Dew

                        

                            Crystal drops sparkle

                            On petals so fair

                            Nature's artwork

                            In morning air
                        

                        
                    

                    

                        
Mountain's Majesty

                        

                            Peaks touch the sky

                            In silent might

                            Ancient giants

                            In morning light
