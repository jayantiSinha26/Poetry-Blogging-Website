Featured Poems

Whispers of the Wind

The wind whispers secrets
Through rustling leaves
A gentle melody
That the heart receives

Moonlit Dreams

Silver beams dance
On midnight's stage
Dreams take flight
As stars engage

Autumn's Song

Golden leaves fall
In a graceful dance
Nature's farewell
To summer's romance

Ocean's Embrace

Waves crash and roll
In endless motion
The sea's deep soul
A timeless ocean

Morning Dew

Crystal drops sparkle
On petals so fair
Nature's artwork
In morning air

Mountain's Majesty

Peaks touch the sky
In silent might
Ancient giants
In morning light

About Poetry Corner

This blog is a collection of thoughts, verses, and reflections – drawn from everyday life, fleeting feelings, and timeless experiences. Whether you're here to read, relate, or simply feel, this space is for anyone who finds comfort in words and beauty in expression.

From free verse to adventurous, love poems to musings on solitude, each post is a journey – some deeply personal, others inspired by the world around us. I believe poetry isn't just art; it's a way of seeing, healing, and connecting.
